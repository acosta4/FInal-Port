About me

I’m Adam Costa. I’m a designer who codes. I’m an Interactive Media Student. I care deeply about creating world-class, useful and beautiful products that help people and make a difference. I like to be involved at different stages of a digital project, from the seed of the idea, through to sketches, design and even the front-end and WordPress build.
